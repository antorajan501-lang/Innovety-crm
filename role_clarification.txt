================================================================================
                    MRF CRM ROLE & PERMISSIONS CLARIFICATION
================================================================================

This document details the responsibilities, actions, and features available for each of the three user roles (ADMIN, TEAM_LEADER, INTERN) defined within the MRF Enterprise CRM.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. ADMIN ROLE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The Admin possesses full site-wide configuration rights and system monitoring tools.

Key Permissions:
- Site Settings: Edit company branding, shift core timing boundaries (deadlines), 
  office GPS geofencing coordinates (Latitude, Longitude), office location names, 
  and allowed ranges.
- Intern Registry: Onboard new interns, modify status, download registry, or bulk import.
- Team Leaders: Onboard team leaders, modify status, or reset credentials.
- Teams Hub: Create workspace teams, edit description, assign leaders, and allocate 
  interns to teams.
- Task Management: Assign tasks to individuals or entire teams. Monitor progress 
  on the timeline backlog and Kanban boards. Update task status, story points, type, 
  priority, and review intern submissions.
- Attendance Audits: Review all system-wide clock-in/out records, verify IPs, 
  browser headers, and reverse-geocoded addresses.
- System Logs: Monitor system-wide operations and audit trails.
- Announcements: Post general system announcements.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. TEAM_LEADER ROLE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The Team Leader manages allocated teams, delegates tasks, and verifies work submissions.

Key Permissions:
- Team Hub: View lead workspace teams, check active members (teammates), review 
  assigned team tasks.
- Task Management: Create and assign tasks to members of their team. Edit task details, 
  story points, priorities, and update status. Review task submissions from interns 
  under their purview (approve or reject).
- Attendance Audits: View attendance logs of interns belonging to their teams.
- Ticket Desk: Raise or resolve support tickets.
- Announcements: Broadcast targeted team announcements.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. INTERN ROLE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The Intern role represents the active team member completing tasks and logging daily attendance.

Key Permissions:
- Dashboard: View task status charts, log in/out widgets, recent notifications.
- Attendance Clock: Clock-in and clock-out daily. Geolocation coordinates and 
  reverse-geocoded address names are validated against the admin's configured 
  office location radius. Includes Google Maps interactive visualization of their 
  current check-in position.
- Team Hub: Access their assigned team workspace, review team details, contact 
  their Team Leader, view teammates, and inspect team tasks.
- Tasks Board: View assigned tasks. Drag tasks between "To Do", "In Progress", 
  and "In Review" statuses. Toggle subtask checklists. Upload work files or submit notes.
- Ticket Desk: Open support tickets for technical or HR queries.
- Announcements: Receive general or team-specific broadcasts.
